Frostbyte: Arctic Sunrise

A multi-purpose Discord bot. Built for you, with love.

This website uses cookies to log in to Discord, and to remember if you closed this. We are legally required to say this. The developers also have no idea how to handle log in sessions without cookies. So, yeah. Cookies.

Why use Frostbyte?

A vast majority of bots on Discord are not intuitive for end users. In addition they are often constrainted to a singular feature , as well as being behind a huge paywall. We understand that this can be of great frustration to server administrators and Discord enthusiasts alike.

Frostbyte aims to eliminate these issues. Being built from the ground up with End Users in mind , Frostbyte is the most streamlined , all-purpouse bot ever. No paywalls , no fiddling with configuration files, no attempting to lure the techies out of the basement, nada. Simplicity at its best.

Where can I use it?

Frostbyte can be utilised on all Discord servers, all you need to do is get an administator to add it and you're all set! Additonaly you may wish to join the Frostbyte Discord Server to get help with Frostbyte or to hang out with other Frostbyte users (we hear they like bamboo). In addition you can test previous and future versions of Frostbyte, with Canary and Legacy versions available. Nifty, huh?
