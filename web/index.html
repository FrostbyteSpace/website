Frostbyte: Arctic Sunrise

A multi-purpose Discord bot. Built for you, with love.

This website uses cookies to log in to Discord, and to remember if you closed this. We are legally required to say this. The developers also have no idea how to handle log in sessions without cookies. So, yeah. Cookies.

But why?

We realised that most Discord bots on Discord are complicated, locked to one feature or heavily paywall locked. And we understand that is infuriating. As of such, we felt there should be an all-purpose bot that is easy to use, easy to set up and easy to understand. And that's what we've done. Frostbyte has been built from the ground up to be easy to use, easy to set up and easy to understand. We've made sure that all of Frostbyte's features are easy to understand, and easier to use. Free of charge. No paywalls. No complicated setup. Just a simple, easy to use bot.

Where can I use it?

You can use Frostbyte on any Discord server. Just add it to your server and you're good to go! You can also join the support server to get help with Frostbyte, or just to hang out with other Frostbyte users. You can also test Canary, Alpha and the Legacy versions of Frostbyte. Nifty, huh?
